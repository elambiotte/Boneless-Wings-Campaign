Why, you may ask, aren't boneless wings the same as chicken nuggets? Let us enlighten you.

More and more people nowadays try to argue that boneless chicken wings are really just the same thing as chicken nuggets. We find this preposterous for a whole host of reasons, the first being that the average boneless wing is clearly much bigger in size than the average chicken nugget.

Secondly, boneless wings are far superior in quality than chicken nuggets.

And finally, boneless wings come tossed in a variety of finger-licking sauces. Most chicken nuggets come with dipping saucecs on the side. They are not evenly coated in flavorful goodness. Due to all of these reasons, boneless wings are a unique delicacy of their own. Not quite chicken nuggets, not quite chicken tenders. Boneless wings forever.
